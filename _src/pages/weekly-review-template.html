Weekly Review Template

A simple Sunday review for turning a messy week into lessons, decisions, and next actions.

Use Template Get Field Notes

The Review

1. What happened?

  • What moved forward?
  • What slipped?
  • What surprised me?

2. What did I learn?

  • What did reality teach me?
  • What assumption changed?
  • What mistake is worth keeping?

3. What matters next?

  • What is the one thing that would make next week meaningful?
  • What should I stop carrying?
  • What needs a calendar block?

4. What gets committed?

  • Top 3 outcomes for the week.
  • One health/non-negotiable.
  • One relationship or adventure action.

Why this exists

The point is not perfect productivity. The point is to notice what your life is actually teaching you before another week buries it.
