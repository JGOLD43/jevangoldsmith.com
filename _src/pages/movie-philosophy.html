My Movie Philosophy

How I choose what to watch, what I look for in a film, and why movies matter to me.

Why I Watch

Movies are the fastest way to live a life that is not mine. The best ones expand my sense of what is possible, sharpen my taste, and leave me with images and questions I cannot shake.

How I Choose What to Watch

I lean on directors I trust, friends with sharp taste, and a small set of critics who write honestly. If a film keeps surfacing in conversations with people I respect, it moves up the queue.

What I Look For

Specificity over spectacle. A clear point of view. Craft in writing, framing, sound, and editing. I would rather watch a small film made with conviction than a big one made by committee.

How I Watch

One film at a time, phone away, lights down. I log every watch on Letterboxd and write a short review when something is worth coming back to. The note matters more than the rating.
