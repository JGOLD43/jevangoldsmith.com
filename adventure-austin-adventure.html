Austin, Texas

Live Music & BBQ Capital

Exploring the heart of Texas

Back to Adventures
Mar 2025
7 days
Austin, Texas

Austin lived up to its reputation as the Live Music Capital of the World. Every night brought new discoveries on 6th Street and beyond.

The BBQ scene here is legendary - from Franklin's to Terry Black's, I sampled brisket that melted in my mouth. Beyond the food and music, the city's tech scene and outdoor activities along Lady Bird Lake made for an unforgettable trip.
