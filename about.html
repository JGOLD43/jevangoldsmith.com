About Me

Finance grad. Writer. Tech enthusiast. RE Development curious.

Background

I'm Jevan Goldsmith, a finance graduate with a passion for real estate development, writing, and technology. My journey has taken me through the worlds of financial analysis, entrepreneurship, and creative expression.

After completing my degree in finance, I discovered that my interests extended far beyond spreadsheets and balance sheets. I became fascinated by how technology is reshaping industries, how great companies build lasting cultures, and how ideas can be communicated effectively through writing.

Writing & Interests

Writing is my way of thinking. Through essays, I explore topics that fascinate me:

  • Philosophy: How we think, why we act, and what drives human behavior in the modern age
  • Management & Culture: What makes organizations thrive or fail, and how leaders can build lasting companies
  • Technology: How innovation shapes our world and creates new opportunities
  • Real Estate Development: The intersection of finance, design, and community building

I'm also an avid reader. You can check out my book reviews to see what I'm currently reading and what I've found valuable.
