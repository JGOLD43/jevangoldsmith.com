Lesson Logger

Insights and lessons I've picked up along the way. Writing them down helps me remember and hopefully helps others too.

2026
February 2026
Mindset

The best time to start was yesterday. The second best time is now.

Spent too long researching the "perfect" way to build this website before realizing I just needed to start. Perfectionism is procrastination in disguise. Ship it, then iterate.

January 2026
Finance

Your lifestyle should lag your income by at least two years

After completing a no-spend month, realized how much lifestyle inflation had crept in. The gap between what you earn and what you spend is what creates freedom.

Learned from: The Psychology of Money
January 2026
Business

Focus on inputs, not outputs

Can't control whether a project succeeds, but can control showing up every day and doing the work. Focusing on process over outcomes reduces anxiety and increases consistency.

2025
December 2025
Relationships

Be the friend who reaches out first

Everyone is waiting for someone else to make the first move. The person who initiates builds the deepest relationships. Don't keep score on who texted last.

November 2025
Life

Travel while you're young and healthy

You can always make more money, but you can't make more time or recapture youth. The experiences and perspectives gained from travel are irreplaceable.

Learned from: Japan trip
October 2025
Mindset

You don't rise to the level of your goals, you fall to the level of your systems

Goals are for setting direction. Systems are for making progress. After failing to stick to vague resolutions, started building specific daily habits instead.

Learned from: Atomic Habits
