Ha Long Bay, Vietnam

Vietnam captivated me from the moment I arrived. The energy of the cities, the serenity of the countryside, and the warmth of the people made this an adventure I'll never forget.

From cruising through the emerald waters of Ha Long Bay to exploring ancient temples in Hoi An, every day brought new wonders. The food alone was worth the trip - fresh pho, banh mi, and countless dishes I'd never heard of before.
